Instructions (Please read first!)

1) Click "Download now" above to download Symplyfy.

2) Once the download finishes, go to your downloads folder (or wherever you saved it to). Right click symplyfy.zip, click extract, and hit okay in the popup window.

3) Open the new folder titled "symplyfy" that was just created. Then open the "symplyfy-win32-ia32" folder.

4) Right click the file named "symplyfy" and click "create shortcut". This will create a file named "Symplyfy - shortcut". Click and drag this icon onto your desktop.

5) Finally, click the new icon named "symplyfy" in your desktop to launch the program!

6) Once Symplyfy is opened, please click on the settings button in the bottom right to finish setting up.

NOTE: Symplyfy is made to replace your desktop environment and doesn't have a "close" button so that it can't be accidentally turned off. The "power down" button on the home screen will turn off the computer, and Symplyfy will automatically relaunch when you turn the computer back on. If you want to manually close Symplyfy without turning off the computer, hold down the "alt" key on your keyboard and press the "f4" key while still holding down the "alt" key. If you have multiple users on your computer, then Symplyfy will only automatically launch on startup for the user who installed it.
