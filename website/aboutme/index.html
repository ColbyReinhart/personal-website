About Me

My name is Colby Reinhart! I was born in Akron Ohio and grew up near Wooster Ohio. I went to school in the Norwayne Local Schools district in Crestion Ohio and I'm currently studying for my BS in Computer Science with a minor in Economics at Kent State University.

I chose computer science as a career path because I've been fascinated with computers from a young age and wanted to learn how they work and how to use them. I love the limitless possibility that computers offer and I'm excited to continue learning long after I graduate and create amazing things with the help of computers!

My hobbies incude coding, video games, working out, and spending time with friends. Although my biggest passion is coding, I am also a powerlifter and focus a lot on my health, strength, and overall physical fitness. I am most proud of my powerlifting achievements but lately I have also been improving my cardio fitness and hope to gain a more well-rounded physique. I also like playing lots of different games on different platforms. Some of my favorite games are Breath of the Wild, Minecraft, Red Dead Redemption 2, God of War 2018, and many more. When I do spend time with friends I prefer to keep my gatherings small. My circle is small but we are close and I care about my friends very much.

When I am coding outside of work, I work on all sorts of different projects. Some of my favorite areas include network/IoT, low/system-level programming, and game programming. Some other areas that I'd like to get into more are robotics and cybersecurity. Making this website is one of my first big projects!
